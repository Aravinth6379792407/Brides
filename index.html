Our Love Story

First Meet

21 March 2021

Once upon a time in a small coffee shop nestled in the heart of the city, Nivetha and Vinoth had their first date. They had met through a mutual friend's introduction and had exchanged a few messages, but this was the first time they would meet in person. Their eyes met, and they exchanged shy smiles as Nivetha approached. She greeted him with a warm hello, and they quickly fell into easy conversation, as if they had known each other for years. The coffee shop's soft jazz music played in the background, providing a comforting ambiance.

First Date

17 Sep 2021

They talked about their favorite books, shared travel stories, and laughed at each other's jokes. Time seemed to fly by as they lost themselves in each other's company. Nivetha noticed the way Vinoth's eyes sparkled when he spoke about his passions, and he couldn't help but admire her infectious laughter. Hours passed, and they realized they had been so engrossed in their conversation that they had forgotten about their drinks, which had long gone cold. They decided to take a stroll in the nearby park, and as they walked hand in hand under the setting sun, it felt like they were the only two people in the world.

Proposal

24 April 2021

Under a starry night sky, in a park illuminated by soft lamplight, Vinoth nervously stammered, "I've loved you since forever, and I want to love you for all our forevers. Will you be mine?" With a tearful smile, Nivetha whispered, "Yes," sealing their love with a sweet kiss.

Engagement

29 June 2023

Amidst a dinner at their favorite venue, Vinoth dropped to one knee, holding an open ring box. With trembling hands and a heart full of love, they said, "I want to spend every day with you, from this moment forward. Will you marry me?" Nivetha eyes welled up with tears of joy as she nodded and whispered, "Yes," and they embraced, sealing their engagement with a promise of forever.
